## What Is an eFoil, Exactly?

An eFoil is a surfboard with an underwater electric motor and a hydrofoil — a wing-shaped fin that generates lift as you gain speed. As the board accelerates, the foil lifts you out of the water entirely. You're flying. Six inches, a foot, eighteen inches above the surface.

The sensation is unlike anything else. No turbulence. No spray. Just silence and speed and a view of the ocean from an angle you've never seen before.

Key specs on ours:
- **Up to 35 km/h** — fast enough to feel alive, controlled enough to learn quickly
- **Silent electric motor** — respectful of the ocean and wildlife
- **60-minute sessions** — enough time to learn the basics and get properly hooked
- **All skill levels** — we mean it, even if you've never been on a board
